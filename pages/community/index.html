Discussions and Meetings

Discussions about Silverblue happen on the desktop@lists.fedoraproject.org mailing list.

Team Silverblue has regular, bi-weekly IRC meetings.

  • #fedora-meeting-2 on freenode, every second Monday, 9:00 EDT
Please check the mailing list for meeting announcements and agendas and feel free to come by!

Outside of the meeting, you can always come by and chat about Silverblue in the #fedora-workstation IRC channel on freenode.

Report Issues

Before reporting issues, please check in chat or on the mailing list that you've found a new issue.

As Team Silverblue covers multiple sub-projects and distributions, where to report issues depends on which distribution or component you're reporting and issue against. In general, it is useful to check on chat or the mailing list where to report things. Here are links to a few of the issue trackers:

  • rpm-ostree on github.
  • flatpak on github.
  • gnome-software on gitlab.gnome.org.

For issues that are specific to Team Silberblue, but not clearly related to one of those components, you can use the Team Silverblue issue tracker.
